What Fortnite parental controls do

Fortnite includes built-in controls that allow parents to manage communication, spending, and gameplay access. 【8-bf0294】

They can control:

  • Voice chat
  • Friend requests
  • Purchases
  • Playtime

How to access parental controls

  1. Open Fortnite or Epic Games account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select “Parental Controls”
  4. Enter or set a PIN
  5. Adjust communication and permissions

A PIN is required to prevent changes without permission. 【9-0c5f31】

Recommended settings for younger children

  • ✅ Voice chat: Friends only or OFF
  • ✅ Friend requests: Restricted
  • ✅ Purchases: Require approval

Why this matters

Parental controls help reduce exposure to inappropriate content and unwanted interactions. 【9-0c5f31】
