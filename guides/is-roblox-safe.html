How to disable voice chat in Roblox

Is Roblox voice chat safe?
  • Safe for kids: ⚠️ Not by default
  • Can strangers talk?: ✅ Yes
  • Should parents change settings?: ✅ Yes

If your child plays Roblox, voice chat can expose them to strangers. Here’s how to disable it quickly and safely.

How to turn off voice chat

  1. Open Roblox
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Click Privacy
  4. Find Voice Chat
  5. Turn it OFF
✅ Tip: You can also block microphone access directly on your device for extra safety.

Recommended settings for kids

  • Turn chat OFF for younger children
  • Allow friends only
  • Use parental PIN protection

Why this matters

Online games allow communication between players, which can include strangers if settings are not restricted.
