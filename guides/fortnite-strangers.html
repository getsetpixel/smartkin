Can strangers talk to my child in Fortnite?

Quick answer
  • Can strangers talk to your child? ✅ Yes
  • Is voice chat on by default? ⚠️ Often yes
  • Can you disable it? ✅ Yes

Yes — strangers can talk to your child in Fortnite if chat settings are not restricted.

How communication works in Fortnite

Fortnite includes both voice and text chat features that allow players to communicate during matches.

  • Voice chat (live conversations)
  • Text chat (messages)

Players can choose who they communicate with, including “Everybody”, friends, or no one at all. 【4-ae5217】

Why this can be a risk

In many matches, players are grouped with people they don’t know, which means children may speak to strangers.

Some guides highlight that communication in games like Fortnite can expose children to unknown players and inappropriate behaviour. 【5-f6d378】

How to stop strangers talking to your child

  1. Open Fortnite settings
  2. Go to Parental Controls
  3. Set Voice Chat to “Friends only” or “Off”
  4. Restrict friend requests
✅ Safest option: Turn chat OFF completely for younger children.

Recommended settings for kids

  • Voice chat: OFF or Friends only
  • Text chat: Restricted
  • Friend requests: Limited

Bottom line

Fortnite is safe only when communication settings are controlled.

Without restrictions, your child can speak to anyone in the same game — including strangers.
