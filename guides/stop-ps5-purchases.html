How to stop purchases on PS5

Quick answer
  • Can kids spend money? ✅ Yes
  • Can you stop it completely? ✅ Yes
  • Best method? ✅ Set spending limit to £0

By default, children can spend money on games and add-ons if payment details are saved on the account.

The safest solution

Use **PlayStation parental controls** to restrict spending.

How to stop PS5 purchases

  1. Go to Settings on your PS5
  2. Select Family and Parental Controls
  3. Open Family Management
  4. Select your child’s account
  5. Set monthly spending limit to £0

Parental controls allow you to fully control purchases and prevent unexpected charges. 【3-fdf872】

Extra safety steps

  • ✅ Remove saved payment methods
  • ✅ Require password for purchases
  • ✅ Use a parent account as “family manager”

Why this matters

Many games include in-game purchases, and children often don’t understand real-world costs. Without restrictions, spending can quickly add up.

⚠️ Tip: Always assume purchases are enabled unless you’ve manually disabled them.

Bottom line

Setting a £0 spending limit is the simplest and safest way to stop PS5 purchases completely.
