    <h1>How to disable Roblox voice chat</h1>

    <section class="quick-answer">
      <ul class="status-list">
        <li class="status-safe">Can voice chat be disabled? Yes</li>
        <li class="status-danger">Is it on by default? No, but can be enabled</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <p>Roblox voice chat allows children to speak directly with strangers. Many parents prefer to disable it.</p>

    <h2>Steps to turn off voice chat</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Open Roblox settings</li>
      <li>Go to Privacy</li>
      <li>Disable voice chat</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Why this matters</h2>
    <p>Voice chat removes the filter of typed messaging and makes real-time conversations harder to control.</p>
